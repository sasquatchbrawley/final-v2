"From the ashes of various bands formed (and disbanded) in the Valley over the past decade comes Guy Fieri Suicide Pact. Part punk, part sludge, part staunch seriousness and equally fiction in the same verse; but always with the audience in mind. These four dudes have been in the audience for longer than they've been in bands... they are aiming to please the music lovers because they are themselves music lovers. So, sit down, relax, and press play. You may just enjoy yourself. Unless you're a hater. Then you don't enjoy anything and you're going to die alone."
